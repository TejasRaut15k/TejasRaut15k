## 🚀 Projects

### KARNA — Kinetic Autonomous Robot for Navigation & Assault
**Duration:** Jul 2025 – Nov 2025  
**Team:** Team Cipher  

KARNA is an intelligent ground robot developed for RoboFest Gujarat 5.0. The robot is designed to autonomously navigate complex and uneven terrains while detecting obstacles and making path decisions using AI-based vision.

The platform also includes surveillance capability and a controlled grenade-ball launching mechanism, demonstrating the integration of mechanical design, autonomous navigation, and intelligent sensing in a single robotic system.

### Drone Base Plate Design
**Duration:** Jun 2025  
**Tool:** Autodesk Fusion 360

Designed a custom structural base plate for a multi-rotor drone. The design focuses on strength-to-weight optimization, balanced geometry, and precise mounting points to support motors and electronic components.

The final model was prepared for manufacturing through CNC machining or laser cutting.

---

### R2 Robot Simulation — ROS2 & Gazebo

Developed a complete robot simulation using ROS2 Jazzy and Gazebo Harmonic. The robot was designed in Fusion 360 and converted into a URDF model for simulation.

The project demonstrates robot modeling, simulation, and differential drive control within a virtual environment, enabling testing of robot movement and behavior through keyboard teleoperation.
